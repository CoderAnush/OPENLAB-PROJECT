Emergency Action Guide

Follow these protocols immediately in case of detected hazards.

Fire / Explosion

1

Trigger the manual fire alarm immediately

2

Evacuate via the nearest safe exit (South Gate preferred)

3

Do NOT use elevators - use stairs only

4

If smoke is present, stay low to the floor

5

Proceed to Assembly Point A (North Parking Lot)

Gas Leak (MQ-2/135)

1

Shut off the main gas valve if safe to do so

2

Open all windows and doors for ventilation

3

Do NOT switch on/off any electrical appliances or lights

4

Evacuate the area immediately (use West Exit)

5

Contact maintenance and fire department immediately

Smoke Inhalation

1

Move to fresh air immediately

2

Cover nose and mouth with wet cloth

3

If victim is unconscious, call 102 (Ambulance)

4

Administer oxygen if trained and available

1

Cut power at the main circuit breaker if safe

3

NEVER use water on electrical fires

4

Evacuate if fire spreads beyond control
